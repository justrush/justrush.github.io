InsightEdge

Co-founder · Strategy & GTM Lead

The AI Virtual COO for service-based small businesses. Service SMBs lose 9–20% of revenue annually — $45K to $115K on a $500K business — to quoting, permitting, job costing, missed rebates, and compliance. Owners burn 20+ hours a week on non-billable admin.

InsightEdge is the first LLM-agnostic, platform-agnostic AI operations layer built for them: connective tissue between the tools they already use (QuickBooks, Housecall Pro), not another system to migrate to. The AI handles the heavy lifting, the owner approves, and it learns to work more autonomously over time. Setup takes minutes — if you can forward an email, you can use InsightEdge.

Where it stands: MVP live with permitting, quoting, and rebate automation. Beta partners across HVAC, Electrical, and Solar are saving 8+ hours per employee per week and surfacing thousands of dollars in untapped rebate value. Targeting a ~$15B market of 3.1M service-based SMBs.

The Long Game

A generational bet on the skilled trades.

In 1919, my great-grandfather emigrated from Ukraine to Tacoma, Washington and founded the Bone-Dry Shoe Company — an enterprise that ran for four decades and employed thousands. That legacy is the blueprint.

America's skilled-trade businesses face a succession crisis: skilled owners retiring with no one to take over. My long-term thesis is to acquire, modernize, and grow these companies — pairing the operational playbooks I built at Amazon with the AI tooling I'm building today, while keeping the crews, the customers, and the community intact.

The target: $1B in cumulative economic contribution to my community by 2050 — through jobs created, businesses preserved, and owners given a real succession path.
